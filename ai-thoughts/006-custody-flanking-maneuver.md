## Risk Mitigation

### 1. Paper Trail
```
• Document the ask: "Custody requested tactical support for Allianz"
• Document the scope: "90 days, specific deliverables"
• Document the approval: "Sponsored by [Name], [Date]"
• If it goes wrong: "I was asked to do this, here's the approval"
```

### 2. Success Criteria
```
• Define clear checkpoints: Day 30, Day 60, Day 90
• Agree on "what does success look like" upfront
• If it's not working at Day 30, pivot or stop
• Don't let it drag on as a zombie project
```

### 3. Graceful Failure Mode
```
• If it fails, own it: "We tried, we learned X, here's what didn't work"
• Don't blame others
• Leave the door open: "The approach is valid, the timing was wrong"
• Maintain relationships for next time
```

### 4. Ally Building
```
• Who in Custody benefits directly if this succeeds?
• Make them look good, give them credit
• They become your defenders when Onboarding Platform attacks
• "It was [Name]'s vision, I just built it"
```
